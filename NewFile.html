This is a page about Cheetahs

Cheetahs can be found in Africa

Here are some more facts about Cheetahs

  • They chirp
  • They are the fasted land animal
  • They have around 2000 spots
  • They use their spotted coats as a disguise
  • The word Cheetah comes from Hindi
  • Only male Cheetahs are social
  • Cheetahs can give birth to 2-8 cubs at a time
